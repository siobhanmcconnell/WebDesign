About

Hello

My name is Siobhan

I'm a student at the University of Montana. I started crocheting in 2024, and I've loved it ever since. I started by learning how to make amigurumi, which is still what I gravitate towards. I'm starting to branch into clothing, but I tend to get too impatient to finish anything. But no matter what I make, I always have fun, so it's my goal to introduce as many people to crochet as possible.
